Fool Pathway

1. What Is The Fool Pathway?

The Fool Pathway is all about fooling perceptions and reality. They specialize in divination, illusions, changing appearances, strong muscle and facial expression control, and turn others into marionettes after taking control of their spirit body threads.

At High Sequences, they can turn their marionettes into duplicates of themselves, bring things (including themselves) from the past to the present, create countless miracles, and create mysterious environments full of tampering and concealment.

Authority: Secrets and Changes | Beyonders: Can only exchange starting at Sequence 3

Above Sequence: Lord of Mysteries | Sefirah: Sefirah Castle | Adjacent Pathways: Door Pathway, Error Pathway

2. Pathway Symbol

Symbol: A Pupil-less Eye and Contorted Lines

Meaning: Represents secrecy and change - the core principles of the Fool Pathway.

Color: Tyrian Purple - a deep, mystical purple that evokes mystery and divine power.

3. Corresponding Tarot Card

The Fool (0 or XXII) is one of the 22 Major Arcana cards, symbolizing a fresh beginning with endless possibilities.

In tarot interpretation, the Fool represents the protagonist embarking on the "Fool's Journey" through the mysteries of life.

This journey is frequently used to introduce beginners to the meaning and significance of all Major Arcana cards.
