Cuttlefish That Loves Diving

Yuan Ye (袁野)

Cuttlefish That Loves Diving is a prolific web novel author and the author of Lord of Mysteries.

His real name is Yuan Ye (袁野). He is from Emeishan City, Sichuan Province. He graduated from College of Computer Science of Sichuan University in 2007. He became a freelance writer in 2013. A member of China Writers Association, Vice Chairman of Sichuan Writers Association (8th), Vice Chairman of Sichuan Network Writers Association (1st).[1]

His representative works include: 《Throne of Magical Arcana》《The Sage Who Transcended Samsara》《灭运图录》《Martial Arts Master》《Lord of Mysteries》《Embers Ad Infinitum》, etc. The 《Throne of Magical Arcana》 pioneered the theme of western fantasy and arcane art, while the 《Lord of Mysteries》 broke the steampunk theme record and was permanently collected by the National Library of China.[1]

Works

His other English-translated works include:

  • 2014~2018: The Sage Who Transcended Samsara (Translation on hiatus)
  • 2016~2018: Martial Arts Master (Completed)
  • 2020~2022: Embers Ad Infinitum (Completed)
  • 2023: Lord of Mysteries 2: Circle of Inevitability (Ongoing)
