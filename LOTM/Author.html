Cuttlefish That Loves Diving

Yuan Ye (袁野)

Pen Name: Cuttlefish That Loves Diving

A prolific and visionary web novel author from Emeishan City, Sichuan Province. Yuan Ye graduated from the College of Computer Science at Sichuan University in 2007 and became a freelance writer in 2013.

Achievements: Member of the China Writers Association | Vice Chairman of Sichuan Writers Association (8th) | Vice Chairman of Sichuan Network Writers Association (1st)

His revolutionary works 《Throne of Magical Arcana》 pioneered western fantasy themes in Chinese web fiction, while 《Lord of Mysteries》 set records in the steampunk genre and was permanently collected by the National Library of China.
