Volume 1: Clown

Quote

"Everyone will die, including me." — Cuttlefish That Loves Diving on Volume 1: Clown

Overview

Clown is the first volume of the Lord of Mysteries series written by Cuttlefish That Loves Diving. It details the story of Klein Moretti as he first arrives in the world of Lord of the Mysteries, his becoming a Beyonder and his adventures with the Nighthawks.

Title Meaning

The meaning of the title Clown refers both to Klein's promotion to Sequence 8 Clown, as well as his inability to withstand the vicissitudes, yet still smiling and continuing along anyway. In this volume, a clown is established to mean those who cannot do anything about their fate, yet continue to smile at it. Despite the many deaths that occur, Klein and the rest of the Nighthawks continue to confront their fates without becoming despondent, fulfilling the meaning of Clown.

Synopsis

Transmigration

In the early morning of June 28, 1349, Klein Moretti committed suicide, leaving a note that read: "Everyone will die, including me." Zhou Mingrui, a history graduate from modern Earth, transmigrates into Klein's body through a Luck Enhancement Ritual, though the inherited memories are incomplete.

Discovery of The Gray Fog

Attempting to return to Earth through the luck ritual, Klein instead discovers The Gray Fog—a mysterious transcendent space. He accidentally summons two fellow Beyonders above this fog: Audrey Hall and Alger Wilson. Recognizing an opportunity, Klein assumes the mysterious persona of "Mr. Fool" and establishes the Tarot Club as a clandestine gathering for sharing information and trading Beyonder ingredients.

Joining the Nighthawks

Klein obtains the Antigonus Family's Notebook, which leads him to join the Blackthorn Security Company—a division of the Nighthawks—as civilian staff. He quickly becomes a Beyonder of the Fool Pathway and, through the Acting Method detailed in Roselle's Diary, advances to Sequence 8: Clown under the Church's guidance.

Conspiracy and Betrayal

During his time with the Nighthawks, Klein experiences a series of unnatural coincidences in rapid succession. Though suspicious, he realizes too late that Ince Zangwill—a mysterious mastermind—has orchestrated everything. Zangwill uses a True Creator's spawn to target Klein with the devastating Sealed Artifact 0-08.

Death and Resurrection

Klein is killed alongside Nighthawk captain Dunn Smith in the confrontation. However, he mysteriously returns from death. Seeking greater strength and advancement, Klein relocates to Backlund, where he begins his quest for revenge while continuing to strengthen himself through the Fool Pathway.
