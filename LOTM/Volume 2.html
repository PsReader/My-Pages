Volume 2: Faceless

Quote

"It was the best of times, it was the worst of times." — Charles Dickens

Overview

Faceless is the second volume of the Lord of Mysteries series. It details Klein Moretti's arrival in Backlund and his new identity as Sherlock Moriarty.

Title Meaning

The title Faceless refers to Klein's promotion to sequence 6 Faceless, as well as the faceless people of East Backlund. It also represents Klein's changing identities and masks.

Synopsis

The Detective Returns

Klein arrives in Backlund, the grand capital city, and establishes himself under a new identity: Sherlock Moriarty, a renowned private detective. This new persona allows him to operate in high society and investigate matters that align with his growing power and influence.

Unraveling Conspiracies

As Sherlock, Klein becomes entangled in various conspiracies and mysteries that plague Backlund. He navigates complex social networks and dangerous intrigue while building his detective career, all while advancing his understanding of the Fool Pathway.

Sequence Advancement

Through his investigations and encounters with mysterious forces, Klein makes significant progress on his spiritual path. He undergoes the trials necessary for the next sequence promotion, becoming increasingly faceless and enigmatic.
